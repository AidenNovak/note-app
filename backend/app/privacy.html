atélier — Privacy Policy

Effective: April 17, 2026 · Last updated: April 17, 2026

atélier ("the app", "we", "us") is a second-brain note and knowledge-graph product published by Jilly. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we store it, and the choices you have. It applies to the iOS app and the supporting backend at backend.jilly.app.

If you only want the summary: we collect the minimum needed to run the app, we store your content in Singapore, we do not sell your data, we let you export or delete everything, and hard deletion completes within 30 days.

1. Data we collect

1.1 Account & identity

  • Email address, display name, and a hashed password (bcrypt).
  • When you use Sign in with Apple, we receive an opaque Apple user identifier and (if you choose) your email or a private relay address.
  • A server-assigned user ID, timestamps, and the device model that last signed in (for security anomaly detection).

1.2 Content you create

  • Notes (text, tags, folders, version history), ideas, and connections in the Mind graph.
  • Audio recordings from voice memos and the transcripts produced from them.
  • Photos or files you explicitly attach to a note.
  • AI-generated insights, summaries, and embeddings derived from the above.
  • Ground posts — notes you choose to share to the community feed, along with likes, reports, and block relationships.

1.3 Usage & device data

  • Crash reports, API error logs, and basic telemetry (endpoint, latency, status code) — no message bodies.
  • Push notification tokens (Expo push) so we can deliver notifications you've opted into.
  • Purchase receipts from Apple / RevenueCat / Stripe webhooks to grant entitlements.

1.4 What we do NOT collect

  • Precise location (no GPS).
  • Contacts, calendar, or health data.
  • Advertising identifiers. We do not run ads and do not share identifiers with ad networks.

2. How we use your data

  • Run the product. Sync notes across devices, build your Mind graph, generate Insights.
  • AI features. Your note content is sent to our AI providers (see §4) to compute embeddings, generate titles and tags, transcribe audio, and produce Insight reports. Providers are contractually bound not to train on your content.
  • Keep the service safe. Rate limiting, fraud checks, moderation of Ground UGC, abuse response.
  • Communicate with you. Transactional email (verification, password reset, receipts). We do not send marketing email without an explicit opt-in.
  • Comply with law. Respond to lawful requests when we have a good-faith basis.

3. Legal bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

  • Contract — to provide the service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interest — fraud prevention, security, minimal diagnostic logging.
  • Consent — optional features like push notifications or cloud audio transcription. Revocable at any time in Settings.
  • Legal obligation — tax records tied to purchases, lawful orders.

4. Third-party processors

We use the following sub-processors. Each processes data only to deliver their service to us:

  • Supabase (Singapore, ap-southeast-1) — primary PostgreSQL database.
  • Railway (Singapore) — API compute (FastAPI).
  • Cloudflare R2 — encrypted object storage for attachments and audio.
  • OpenRouter / Moonshot Kimi — chat and embedding model inference.
  • OpenAI Whisper — audio transcription.
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, Push (APNs), In-App Purchases.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management for iOS IAP.
  • Stripe — web payment processing.
  • Resend — transactional email.
  • Expo — OTA updates and push relay.

5. Data residency & cross-border transfers

Your account data and content live in Singapore. Some processors (e.g. OpenAI, Stripe) operate globally; when content is sent to them it may transit their US/EU infrastructure. Transfers from the EU/UK rely on Standard Contractual Clauses. If you're in mainland China, your data still resides in Singapore; cross-border transfer is a necessary part of providing the service.

6. Retention & deletion

  • You can delete any individual note, recording, or post at any time from within the app.
  • Account deletion (Profile → Delete Account, or DELETE /auth/account) starts a 30-day soft-delete window during which your account is inaccessible but recoverable.
  • After 30 days an automated sweeper permanently erases your user row, notes, embeddings, Ground posts, likes, reports, blocks, OAuth identities, and R2 attachments.
  • Billing receipts may be retained for tax/accounting reasons for up to 7 years in a separate system.

7. Your rights

Regardless of jurisdiction, we honor the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of your data.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion (see §6).
  • Portability — export notes as Markdown/JSON.
  • Objection — opt out of non-essential processing.
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any right email 2453204059@qq.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Security

  • TLS 1.2+ in transit; AES-256 at rest on Supabase and R2.
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt; session tokens are signed JWTs with short lifetimes.
  • Principle of least privilege for staff access; audit logs on admin actions.
  • No production credentials stored in the mobile bundle.

9. Children

atélier is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, email us and we will remove it.

10. AI-generated content

Insights, auto-titles, and tags are generated by large language models. Output can be incorrect or biased. We label AI-generated surfaces in the app and you should not rely on them for medical, legal, or financial decisions.

11. Community / Ground

Content you share to Ground is visible to other atélier users. We offer in-app tools to report content, block users, and hide posts. We review reports and remove violating content within 24 hours. Repeated violations lead to account termination.

12. Changes

Material changes will be announced in-app and by email at least 14 days before taking effect. The current version is always at backend.jilly.app/privacy.
