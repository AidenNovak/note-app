The Process

From Capture to Insight

atelier transforms your scattered thoughts into a structured knowledge system through four simple steps.

1

Capture Everything

Quickly capture thoughts through text, voice, images, or web clips. No friction, no formatting required. Just get your ideas out of your head and into the system.

2

Automatic Organization

atelier normalizes your inputs into structured Markdown, extracts tags, and builds connections. The knowledge graph grows automatically as you add more notes.

3

AI-Powered Synthesis

AI analyzes patterns across your notes, identifying themes, connections, and insights you might have missed. Get actionable suggestions based on your actual thinking patterns.

4

Refine and Reuse

Transform raw captures into polished notes while maintaining links to original fragments. Your refined knowledge stays connected to its source material.
